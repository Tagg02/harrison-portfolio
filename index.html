Carbon Footprint Calculator (Demo)

This demo estimates your annual carbon footprint (tCO₂e/year) based on a few simple inputs: weekly driving, monthly electricity use, flights per year, and diet category. It’s intentionally lightweight and runs entirely in the browser — no tracking, no accounts, and no stored data.

How the estimate works: each input is multiplied by a simplified emissions factor, then combined into a total annual estimate. The purpose here is portfolio demonstration: clear calculations, user-friendly form design, and readable results.

Note: This is not a scientific audit. Emissions factors vary by country, energy mix, vehicle type, and more. If you want, this can be upgraded to use UK-specific published factors and include a clearer breakdown + charts.
